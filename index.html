Hi, I'm Kai.

My pronouns are he/they. I am an incoming Ph.D. student at UCLA's Department of Information Studies, where I will be advised by Dr. Safiya Noble. My current research interests include the ways dominant data systems impact trans and gender nonbinary communities of color, as well as the development of grassroots and community-based interventions and technologies to resist violence and build new futures.

I received my Master of Information and Data Science from UC Berkeley's School of Information and my B.A. in Asian American and Asian Diaspora Studies from UC Berkeley.
